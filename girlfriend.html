WHIPLASH!


		With a touch so gentle, yet fierce and bold,
		She speaks to me in rhythms untold.
		Her voice, a thunderous roar or a soft caress,
		In every beat, she brings forth my best.
                                                                          
		I listen to her heartbeat, steady and true,
		In her rhythm, I find myself anew.
		Her sound, a jazz tune, so smooth and syncopated,
		In every note, my heart finds its beat.
